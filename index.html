Services

We take pride in caring for each dog as our own. Choose from our range of services to pamper your pooch

Full Groom

Includes a pre-groom, 2 shampoo washes, blow dry, full body styling, eyes and ears cleaned and nails clipped

Spa Treatment

Treat your pooch to our hydrating Argan oil conditioning treatment and wildwash paw and nose balm

Wash & Go

Full use of all our facilities for 30 minutes, including our full-height bath, professional drier, microfibre towels and even cologne

Paw & Nails

Allow our groomers to trim your dogs nails. We file the nails too so they are neater, smoother and safer.

Ear Cleaning

Ears checked and wax removed. Ear hair trim as required

Bath & Brush

This includes a wash with 2 shampoos, dry, nail clipping, ear cleaning and a brush
